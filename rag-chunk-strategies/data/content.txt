Badminton Court Booking and Usage Policy

Court Booking Rules:
Players can book badminton courts up to 7 days in advance. Each booking must be for a minimum of 30 minutes and a maximum of 2 hours. Players are allowed to make up to 3 active bookings at any given time.

Cancellation Policy:
Bookings can be cancelled up to 12 hours before the scheduled time for a full refund. If a cancellation is made within 12 hours of the booking, no refund will be issued. Repeated late cancellations may result in temporary suspension of booking privileges.

Player Limits:
Each badminton court allows a maximum of 4 players at a time. Doubles matches are encouraged during peak hours to maximize court utilization.

Peak and Off-Peak Hours:
Peak hours are defined as weekdays from 6 PM to 10 PM and weekends from 8 AM to 10 PM. Higher pricing applies during peak hours. Off-peak hours offer discounted rates.

Check-in Rules:
Players must check in at least 10 minutes before their scheduled booking time. Failure to check in within 10 minutes after the start time may result in automatic cancellation of the booking.

Equipment Policy:
Players must use non-marking shoes on the court. Bringing outside shuttlecocks is allowed, but only feather shuttlecocks are permitted for matches.

Coaching Sessions:
Coaching sessions can be booked separately and are limited to a maximum of 6 participants per session. Coaches must be certified and approved by the facility.

Code of Conduct:
Players must maintain proper sportsmanship and respect other players. Any form of misconduct or damage to the court may result in penalties or bans.

Membership Benefits:
Members receive priority booking access and a 10% discount on all bookings. Premium members can book courts up to 14 days in advance.
